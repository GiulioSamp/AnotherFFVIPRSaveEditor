Fix save corruption caused by stripped base64 padding

SaveCrypto.Encrypt removed the trailing = padding from the base64
output. The reference save used to derive the framing happened to be
a length that needed no padding, which made the strip look correct.
In reality the game writes standard padded base64 (verified against
game-written saves) and rejects unpadded input, so roughly two out of
three editor writes were corrupted: every write whose ciphertext
length was not a multiple of 3.

This was the root cause behind the intermittent save-data-corrupted
reports across v1.1.0 through v1.1.4. The failures tracked payload
size, not the content being edited, which is why the bug masqueraded
as several different ones (stat edits, equipment changes, inventory
removal, quicksave slots). Verified in-game with a no-op rewrite and
with previously failing edits.
